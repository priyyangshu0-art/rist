How to Apply

  • Fill the online application form with personal, academic, and contact details.
  • Upload required documents (PDF/JPG as per instructions).
  • Submit the form. You will receive an acknowledgment via email.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for counseling/verification.
  • Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.

Eligibility

Diploma (Polytechnic)

Passed Class 10 (Madhyamik or equivalent) with Mathematics & Science. State-level entrance (if applicable) will be considered.

Lateral Entry

Passed Class 12 (Vocational/Science) or ITI (2 years) in relevant trade as per applicable Board norms.

Reservation

As per Government/Board norms. Candidates must produce valid certificates during verification.

Documents Required

  • Class 10 Marksheet & Certificate
  • Class 12 / ITI Marksheet (for Lateral)
  • Transfer/Migration Certificate
  • Category/Reservation Certificate (if applicable)
  • Photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport)
  • Recent Passport Photos
  • Entrance Rank/Allotment (if applicable)
  • Address Proof
  • Any other as notified

Fees & Scholarships

Tuition & Other Fees

As per institute policy. Detailed fee structure will be provided during counseling. Contact admissions for latest updates.

Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships and Government schemes are available for eligible students. Early applicants may get priority.

Education Loans

Support for documentation is provided. Contact the Admissions Office for bank tie-ups and letters.

FAQs

How will I know my application is received?

You will get an acknowledgment email after submission. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for next steps.

Can I edit my form after submission?

If you need to change critical information, contact the Admissions Office with your application ID.

Is there a hostel?

Hostel support is available subject to availability. Contact the office for application details.
